Classificação Etária

Classificação da App Store: 9+ · Última atualização: 20 de abril de 2026 · Español

Short version: MyPetLogger is a private pet-care journal designed for pet owners of any age. Apple's App Store rates it 9+ because the app contains Infrequent/Mild Medical or Treatment Information — specifically, records and reminders for vaccinations, medications, dewormings, flea treatments, and veterinary visits for pets. It is suitable for children 9 and older, and is appropriate for family use under typical adult supervision.

1. Por que a classificação 9+

MyPetLogger lets owners keep a complete medical history for each of their pets. Because the app displays pet-related medical and treatment terminology — for example "rabies vaccine," "dewormer," "flea treatment," or "next vet visit" — Apple's App Store rating system classifies this as Infrequent/Mild Medical/Treatment Information. Per Apple's guidelines, that single descriptor results in a 9+ rating, even though the content is purely informational, factual, and pet-focused.

2. Conteúdo que o app NÃO contém

To help parents, guardians, and reviewers make an informed decision, MyPetLogger contains none of the following:

  • No violence, cartoon or otherwise.
  • No realistic or suggestive imagery.
  • No profanity, crude humor, or mature themes.
  • No horror, fear, or distressing content.
  • No depiction or promotion of alcohol, tobacco, or drugs.
  • No gambling, simulated or otherwise.
  • No unrestricted web browsing inside the app.
  • No user-generated content, social feeds, chat, or messaging features.
  • No in-app communication between users.

3. Publicidade

The free tier shows ads via Google AdMob, configured for a general audience. Ad content is filtered to exclude age-inappropriate categories. Users on any paid tier (Ad-Free, Premium Annual, or Lifetime) see no ads at all.

4. Data & privacy

MyPetLogger does not require an account, does not collect personal data about you or your pets, and stores all pet records on-device (with optional sync through your own iCloud). For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

5. Controles parentais

If you wish to restrict access for a younger user, you can use Apple's built-in Screen Time controls to limit installs by App Store rating, restrict in-app purchases, or disable advertising identifiers.

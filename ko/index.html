왜 이것을 만들었는가

개발자의 메모.

I had been meaning to organize my dogs' vaccination cards for about three years. The paperwork lived in a folder on my desk, a second folder in a drawer, a Google Drive with conflicting filenames, three notes on my phone, and my memory — which was the least reliable archive of the five. Every time I went to the vet, I re-discovered the same facts, printed the same printouts, and re-wrote the same dates on the same forms.

The apps I found either wanted me to make an account, or wanted to show me ads for kibble, or wanted to sell me a subscription for a feature that should have been basic. None of them felt like the kind of thing you'd open on a quiet Sunday and be glad to sit with for ten minutes.

So I built MyPetLogger — for myself first, and for anyone who's ever stood at a vet's counter thumbing through a phone trying to find the exact word "Bordetella." It's SwiftUI top to bottom. It uses the Apple frameworks the way they want to be used. It's translated into nine languages. It syncs through your iCloud, not ours. And it's free up to two pets — not as a trial, but as a permanent tier, because most households really do have one or two companions and they deserve a good home too.

If you try it and it feels right, send a note. If it feels wrong, send a louder one. This app gets better because people tell me what they need.

— Victor, LC&V Systems

자주 묻는 질문

질문에 답합니다.

어떤 반려동물을 지원하나요?
All of them. Dogs and cats are first-class citizens, but the species picker also covers birds, rabbits, ferrets, hamsters, guinea pigs, reptiles, fish, and a free-text fallback for anything truly unusual. Each species gets its own breed list, color palette, and icon — and every pet, regardless of species, gets the full medical timeline, reminders, and quick actions.
Android 버전이 있나요?
Not today. MyPetLogger is built in SwiftUI to feel deeply native on Apple's platforms — Live Activities, Dynamic Island, Siri Intents, HealthKit, WatchConnectivity. We'd rather do one platform well than two platforms badly.
계정이 필요한가요?
No. There's no sign-up, no email, no password. Open the app and start. If you turn on iCloud 동기화, your records move between your own devices through your own Apple ID — never through us.
저에 대해 실제로 무엇을 알고 있나요?
Almost nothing. We can see anonymous crash reports if you've opted into Apple's analytics, and we see aggregate download counts from the App Store. We have no database of users, no list of pet names, no way to contact you unless you email us first.
제 데이터를 내보낼 수 있나요?
Yes. Premium unlocks full PDF 내보내기 per pet — perfect for a new vet, an insurance claim, or just a keepsake. The data itself lives in Core Data on-device, so you're never locked in.
프리미엄을 취소하면 어떻게 되나요?
Your data is yours forever. If you drop back to the 무료 tier, existing pets above the two-pet limit remain visible and editable, but you'll need to remove one before adding a new one.
광고를 사용하나요?
무료 users see occasional banners and an interstitial after every third log entry. Paid users — Ad-무료, Premium, 평생 — see zero ads, ever. Ads never appear inside log forms or on the Map tab.
iPad에서 작동하나요?
Yes. iPad gets the same tab bar as iPhone via SwiftUI's sidebar-adaptable TabView, so every screen is reachable and the layout expands gracefully.
웹 앱이 있나요?
No, and there won't be. "Web app" means "data on our servers," and our data is on your phone. If we ever made one, this page would be different.
